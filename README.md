# 🎯 Thynk Unlimited - AR Member Card Experience

Web-based Augmented Reality experience for club member cards using MindAR.js + A-Frame.

## 🌐 Live Demo

**URL:** https://rolex-2121.github.io/ar-clubcard/

## 📱 How to Use

1. **Scan the QR code** on your physical member card with your phone camera
2. **Allow camera permissions** when prompted by the browser
3. **Point your phone** at the member card design
4. **Watch the video** appear and stick to the card in 3D space!

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **MindAR.js** - Image tracking library
- **A-Frame** - WebXR framework
- **GitHub Pages** - Free HTTPS hosting
- **Web-AR** - No app installation required
